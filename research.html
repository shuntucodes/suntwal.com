Research areas

Research

I work in artificial intelligence, especially natural language processing, and in organizational cybersecurity. These are separate research streams. I also study how people interpret, assess, and act on information under uncertainty.

Transferable and interpretable AI

I study methods that help AI models, especially natural language processing systems, generalize across domains. This includes work on domain transfer, fact verification, and interpretable models.

Human judgment and online information

I study how people assess and share online information, including the roles of verbal and nonverbal cues and algorithmic decision aids under uncertainty.

Organizational cybersecurity

Separately from my natural language processing work, I study technology risk and cybersecurity decision-making in organizations, including sector-specific vulnerabilities and control prioritization.
