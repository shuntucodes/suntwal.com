Welcome. I am an Assistant Professor of Information Systems at the University of Colorado Colorado Springs.

My research has two distinct streams: artificial intelligence, particularly natural language processing, and organizational cybersecurity. I also study how people assess online information and use algorithmic decision aids under uncertainty.

This site collects my publications, selected presentations, teaching, and other academic materials.
